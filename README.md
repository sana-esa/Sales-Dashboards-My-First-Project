
Esa'S Fashion Project: 2022 Annual Sales Analysis

This repository contains the data analysis and key findings for Esa'S Fashion's sales performance in the year 2022. The analysis aims to provide actionable insights into customer demographics, geographic sales distribution, channel performance, and order status.

Key Findings (Based on Data Snippets)

Initial analysis of the provided data snippets reveals several critical areas of performance:

Gender Dominance: Women significantly drive the sales, accounting for a larger portion of the total sales amount compared to Men (e.g., Women: $13,562,773$ vs. Men: $7,613,604$).

Top Channels: Amazon and Myntra appear to be the dominant sales channels, capturing the highest percentage of total orders.

Geographic Focus: Sales are highly concentrated in the top 5 states, with MAHARASHTRA and KARNATAKA leading the contribution to the total sales amount.

Customer Demographics: The Adult age group is the largest demographic segment across both genders.

Monthly Trends: Sales and order counts appear to peak around the March/April period and generally decline toward the end of the year.

Fulfillment Success: The vast majority of orders have been successfully Delivered, with relatively low cancellation, return, and refund rates.

Suggested Next Steps

The next phase of analysis should focus on:

Calculating key performance indicators (KPIs) like Average Order Value (AOV) by channel and demographic.

Analyzing product categories or SKUs to determine best-selling items.

Developing strategies to increase sales in underperforming months (e.g., Nov, Dec) and states.
